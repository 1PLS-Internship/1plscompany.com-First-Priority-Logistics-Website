About Us

Built on decisive action at the port, road, and yard.

First Priority Logistics Company began as a small dispatch group serving hotels along Manila Bay. Over a decade, we invested in container handling equipment, trained compliance officers, and built a culture that treats every shipment like it is the first priority.

Today we orchestrate freight and customs workflows for hospitality chains, food distributors, and retailers who need shipments cleared and delivered without the drama.

"Logistics is an act of stewardship. When we carry what matters to people, we carry their trust." – Founder, Arman Del Rosario
